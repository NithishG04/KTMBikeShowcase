RC

The KTM RC is a popular line of motorcycles manufactured by KTM, an Austrian company known for its performance-oriented bikes. The Duke series is especially favored for its sporty design, sharp handling, and powerful engines, making it a top choice for riders who love street and naked bikes. Below is a detailed explanation of the KTM Duke series:

The KTM RC series offers a range of sport bikes to suit different riders, from beginners to seasoned track enthusiasts. Each model is designed with KTM's "Ready to Race" philosophy, emphasizing lightweight construction, high-performance engines, and advanced features. Whether you're starting with the RC 125 or pushing the limits on the RC 8C, the RC series delivers an exhilarating riding experience.

Variant

  1. Rc 125
    • A entry-level sport bike designed for beginners or riders with A1 licenses (in Europe).
    • Features a 125cc single-cylinder engine.
  2. Rc 200
    • A step up from the RC 125, with a 200cc single-cylinder engine.
    • Offers a balance of performance and affordability, popular in markets like India and Asia.
  3. Rc 390
    • The most popular model in the RC series, featuring a 373cc single-cylinder engine.
    • Known for its excellent power-to-weight ratio, making it a favorite among enthusiasts for both street and track use.
  4. Rc 8R
    • A limited-production, track-only model designed for serious racing enthusiasts.
    • Powered by a 889cc parallel-twin engine derived from the KTM 890 Duke.
    • Features premium components like Brembo brakes, WP Pro suspension, and a lightweight chassis.
