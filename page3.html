DUKE

The KTM Duke is a popular line of naked street motorcycles produced bythe Austrian manufacturer KTM. Known for their aggressive styling, lightweight construction, and powerful engines, the Duke series has gained a strong following among riders who appreciate performance-oriented bikes. Here’s an overview of the KTM Duke lineup:

For more detial click this video

Variant

  1. KTM Duke 125
    • A beginner-friendly bike with a 125cc single-cylinder engine.
    • Ideal for new riders or those with an A1 license (in Europe).
  2. KTM Duke 200
    • A step up from the 125 Duke, with a 200cc engine offering more power and torque.
    • Popular in markets like India and Southeast Asia.
  3. KTM Duke 250
    • A lightweight and nimble bike with a 250cc single-cylinder engine.
    • Great for urban commuting and occasional highway rides.
  4. KTM Duke 390
    • One of the most popular models in the lineup, featuring a 373cc single-cylinder engine.
    • Known as a "pocket rocket" due to its impressive performance and affordability.
  5. KTM Duke 790
    • A mid-range naked bike with a 799cc parallel-twin engine.
    • Offers a balance of power, agility, and advanced electronics.
  6. KTM Duke 890/R
    • A more powerful version of the 790 Duke, with an 889cc parallel-twin engine.
    • The "R" version is track-focused, with upgraded suspension and brakes.
  7. KTM Super Duke 1290/R
    • The flagship model of the Duke series, featuring a 1301cc V-twin engine.
    • Known as "The Beast," it delivers insane power and torque, making it one of the most powerful naked bikes on the market.
  8. KTM Super Duke 1390/R Evo
    • The latest flagship model, introduced in 2024, with a 1350cc V-twin engine and even more advanced electronics and performance features.
